Areas of Interests

His areas of interest in Pain medicine are:

Musculoskeletal pain arising from large joints (hips, knees and shoulders) and spine.

Neuropathic pain disorders

Fibromyalgia, CFS, ME

Cancer pain

Dr Alkholany adopts a holistic approach for the management of his chronic pain patients aiming at improving quality of life and regaining functionality of his patients. Within that approach, he applies interventional procedures and pharmacologic management as indicated hand in hand with the help of physiotherapy and pain psychology whenever indicated to achieve optimum outcomes for his patients and helping them to regain the quality of life they are aspiring to. He also adopts a trauma informed care to his patients whenever there is an element of psychologic trauma as part of the chronic pain disorder.

Interventions offered

Wide Range of ultrasound guided and X ray guided interventional procedures

For musculoskeletal pain related to large joints (Shoulders, Hips and Knees) and Spine

Shoulder pain

US guided suprascapular and Axillary nerve block and Pulsed radiofrequency for shoulder pain.

US guided shoulder joint hydro dilation, AC joint injection, subacromial injection for shoulder pain.

X ray guided diagnostic suprascapular, Axillary and lateral pectoral nerve block and conventional RF denervation.

Knee pain

X ray guided diagnostic knee genicular branch block and conventional RF denervation for chronic knee pain.

US guided knee joint injection.

Hip pain

Combined X ray/US guided diagnostic hip articular branch block and conventional RF denervation for chronic hip pain.

US guided trochanteric bursa injection for greater trochanteric syndrome.

US guided hip joint injection.

Neck and low back pain

X ray guided cervical facet joint injection and conventional radiofrequency for neck pain.

X ray guided lumbar diagnostic Medial branch facet joint block and conventional RF denervation for low back pain.

X ray guided SIJ injection for low back pain.
