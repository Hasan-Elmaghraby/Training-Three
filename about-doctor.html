About-us

Dr Mahmoud Alkholany is a consultant in pain medicine and Anaesthesia at Liverpool University Hospitals.

Dr Alkholany is privileged to have gained his training and experience across three different health care systems.

He qualified in 2005 from a prestigious medical school in Cairo, Egypt at Ain Shams University. He finished the residency program in Anaesthesia, Critical care and Pain medicine and gained a Master of science in Anaesthesia after which he was appointed as a teaching assistant in the same University.
